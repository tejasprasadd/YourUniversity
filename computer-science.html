Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computing and computational systems. The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program provides students with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively design, develop, and maintain complex computer systems.

Program Requirements

  • Completion of 120 credit hours

  • Minimum GPA of 2.0

  • Core courses in computer science, mathematics, and statistics

  • Elective courses in areas such as artificial intelligence, software engineering, and cybersecurity

  • Senior capstone project

>Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program are prepared for careers in a variety of industries, including:

  • Software development

  • Network engineering

  • Web development
